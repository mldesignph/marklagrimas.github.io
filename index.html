About Me

Design that sells.
Work that lasts.

I'm Mark Lagrimas, a Creative Supervisor and Graphic Designer with over 8 years of experience crafting impactful visual solutions across digital and print media. I currently lead the creative team at Nexus Ingredients, Inc., overseeing brand identity, marketing collateral, social media content, and event visuals for multiple client brands.

Before stepping into supervision, I spent over 4 years as Graphic Designer II at Megasoft Hygienic Products Inc., managing packaging for major FMCG brands — including Sisters, Fasclean, Cherub, Twins Lampein, and Mega Soft Tissue — and navigating the full ASC approval process end-to-end.

Grounded in Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva, with a Computer Science background that gives me a technical edge in digital environments — including WordPress development and AI design tools.

Where vision
meets execution

My approach to creative leadership is grounded in clarity and craft. As Creative Supervisor at Nexus Ingredients, Inc., I've learned that great work doesn't happen by accident — it happens when the team has a clear brief, the right tools, and someone who protects the quality at every stage.

I lead by doing. I'm in the files alongside my team, holding the same standard for my own output as for everyone I work with. Whether it's a tradeshow backdrop, a social post, or a product launch package — every touchpoint gets the same care.

How I Work

Brief to brilliant,
step by step

01
Understand
Study the brief, brand, audience, and shelf or screen context before touching any tool.
02
Concept
Explore multiple visual directions. Moodboard and reference — commit to the strongest idea.
03
Execute
Design with precision in Illustrator and Photoshop. Every detail — colour, type, spacing, finish.
04
Review
Present with context. Incorporate feedback efficiently. No ego — just the best possible output.
05
Deliver
Supply press-ready files, mockups, and digital assets. Clean, organised, ready to deploy.
